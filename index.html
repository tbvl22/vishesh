Vishesh Kumar

PhD Scholar @ Trustworthy BiometraVision Lab, IISER Bhopal

Adversarial Patches • Attacks • Defenses

Vishesh Kumar is a dedicated PhD scholar in the Trustworthy BiometraVision Lab at IISER Bhopal, specializing in the security of biometric systems. His research focuses on designing and evaluating adversarial patches to understand vulnerabilities in face and fingerprint recognition, crafting novel attack algorithms to stress-test deep-learning models, and developing robust defense strategies that enhance system resilience. With multiple publications in top-tier conferences and journals, Vishesh actively collaborates across computer vision and security communities to advance trustworthy biometric technologies.
